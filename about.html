The FCRIT Legacy

Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Institute of Technology (FCRIT), established in 1994, has always been at the forefront of technical education and holistic student development. FACES, our annual sports festival, is more than just a competition; it is a celebration of discipline, teamwork, and the indomitable spirit of Agnelites.

Why Participate in FACES 2026?

  • 🔹 Showcase Talent: Compete with the best athletes in the college.
  • 🔹 Build Network: Meet like-minded sports enthusiasts and build lasting friendships.
  • 🔹 Premium Experience: Enjoy professionally managed matches with official referees and live score updates.
  • 🔹 Glorious Prizes: Win medals, certificates, and substantial cash prizes.

"Sports do not build character, they reveal it."
